Colombia

An Unexpected Delight

In September 2015, my wife won us a holiday of a lifetime to a country known more for it's violence and drug-fueled underworld, we didnt know what to expect...

But after seven days in this amazing place - my opinion would be changed forever!

Bogota

A city steeped in history but striding into the modern age

Places to check out include:

  • The Catedral located in Bolivar Sq.
  • Downtown Bogota
  • Monserrate
  • plus many more...

Cartagena

An historic paradise

Bask on the Caribbean Coast, either spending time visiting the old colonial city or lounging on the sandy beaches.
